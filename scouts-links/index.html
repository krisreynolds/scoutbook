    Systems and tools¶

    Scouts.org.uk¶

    The homepage of The Scout Association - a gateway to the various systems and information available.

    My Membership¶

    The replacement for Compass, sometimes referred to as SWAN (System Without A Name). Manage your details, disclosures training, reviews and more. Team Leaders can also manage their team members.

    My Learning¶

    This is where you go to complete your mandatory and optional training.

    Digital Skills Courses¶

    Want to improve your digital skills? Find some handy guides here to help you improve your IT skills.

    Policy, Organisation & Rules (POR)¶

    The rules of Scouting that we all need to follow. This is updated regularly and is the place to go if you've got any questions about what is and isn't allowed.
