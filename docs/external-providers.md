## Duke of Edinburgh's Award

### [Expeditions Wales](https://www.expeditionswales.co.uk/)
Expeditions Wales is a friendly organisation that prides itself on delivering a quality service, focusing on the safety and enjoyment of its clients throughout. Expeditions Wales is licensed by the Adventurous Activities Licensing Service to provide Hill Walking and Trekking activities and is an Approved Activity Provider for The Duke of Edinburgh's Award Expedition section.
